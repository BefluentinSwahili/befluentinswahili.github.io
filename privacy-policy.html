Privacy Policy

Your Privacy Matters

At Befluent Swahili, we are committed to protecting your privacy. We collect site usage data using Google Analytics to enhance your browsing experience. Additionally, when you sign up for our services or engage in transactions, we may collect your full name, email address, and country for communication and billing purposes. We also offer payment options through PayPal and Payoneer, ensuring secure transactions. Your personal information is kept confidential and will not be shared with third parties. Furthermore, our one-on-one Zoom sessions with tutors are conducted in a private and secure environment to safeguard your privacy. We respect your trust and strive to maintain the highest standards of confidentiality and security. If you have any concerns or questions about our privacy practices, please contact us at habari@befluentswahili.com or use the form on the contact page to send us a quick message.
